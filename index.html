About me

My hobby is programming. I worked in a related field where I was involved in data analysis in the field of unmanned aerial vehicles for 2 years. My transition to data science from business analytics opened up incredibly interesting tools for solving non-standard problems for me. When I started my data science journey, I had very little knowledge in programming and for a general understanding of the basics, I learned C ++. Then I mastered Python and now I write a variety of projects on it.

Projects
